IQRA UNIVERSITY COMPREHENSIVE INFORMATION DATABASE

---
1. ABOUT IQRA UNIVERSITY
- Motto: "Commanding Knowledge"
- Vision: To be a world-class institution of higher education and research.
- Status: Chartered by the Government of Sindh, recognized by HEC (Higher Education Commission) with the highest category 'W'.
- Chancellor: Mr. Hunaid H. Lakhani (Founding Chancellor).

---
2. CAMPUSES (KARACHI & OTHERS)
- Main Campus (Defence View): Located near the Expressway. The hub for Engineering, CS, and Business Administration.
- Gulshan Campus: Located in Gulshan-e-Iqbal, Abul Hassan Isphahani Road.
- North Campus: Located in North Nazimabad.
- Bahria Town Campus: The newest state-of-the-art campus.
- Islamabad Campus: Focuses on Social Sciences and Fashion Design.

---
3. ADDMISSION CRITERIA & PROCESS
- Undergraduate (BS/BBA):
   - Minimum 50% marks in Intermediate (HSSC) or equivalent (A-Levels require equivalence certificate from IBCC).
   - Aptitude Test mandated by the university must be cleared.
   - Interview performance.
- Graduate (MBA/MS):
   - 16 years of education with minimum 2.0/4.0 CGPA or 50% marks.
   - GAT (General) or IU Admission Test.
- PhD:
   - 18 years of education (MS/MPhil) with minimum 3.0/4.0 CGPA.
   - GAT (Subject) or GRE required.

---
4. FEE STRUCTURE (ESTIMATES - SUBJECT TO CHANGE)
- Admission Fee: ~Rs. 20,000 (One time, non-refundable).
- Semester Registration: ~Rs. 10,000 per semester.
- Tuition Fees (Per Credit Hour):
   - BBA / BS CS: ~Rs. 6,500 - 7,500 per credit hour.
   - MBA: ~Rs. 8,000 per credit hour.
   - Fashion Design: Slightly higher due to studio costs.
- Scholarships:
   - Merit Scholarship: Up to 50-100% tuition waiver for high GPA maintainers (3.5+).
   - Need-Based: For financially challenged students.
   - Kinship: For siblings studying simultaneously.

6. GRADING & EXAMS
- Grading Scale: absolute grading is often used, converted to GPA.
   - A  (87-100) : 4.0
   - B+ (80-86) : 3.5
   - B  (72-79) : 3.0
   - C+ (66-71) : 2.5
   - C  (60-65) : 2.0
   - F  (Below 60) : Fail
- Attendance Policy: Strict 80% attendance is required to sit in the final exam.
- "I" Grade: Incomplete (due to valid reasons, must be cleared next semester).
- Probation: GPA below 2.0 leads to probation. Two consecutive probations may lead to suspension.

7. FACILITIES
- Digital Library: Access to IEEE Xplore, JSTOR, and HEC Digital Library.
- Transport: Shuttle service available from major points in Karachi (with monthly pass fees).
- Cafeteria: Hygienic food courts at all campuses.
- Sports Complex: Gym, Table Tennis, Cricket ground access.
- Incubation Center: BIC (Business Incubation Center) for student startups.

8. HOW TO APPLY FOR TRANSCRIPT / DEGREE
- Apply via the online LMS portal.
- Normal tracking: 15-20 working days.
- Urgent tracking: 5-7 working days (Extra fees apply).
- Degree clearance requires submission of transcript fee, library clearance, and alumni form.
